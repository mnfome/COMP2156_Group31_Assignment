George Brown College (GBC) is a public college in Toronto, Canada, offering a wide range of diplomas, degrees, and certificates in various fields like business, technology, health sciences, and hospitality.
Some of the IT programmes of George Brown College are Computer Systems Technician, Computer Programming and Analysis , Cyber Security.
George Brown College Location: this college has 3 campuses and all of them are in downtown, the main Campus which is specially for IT is the CASALOMA Campus.
George Brown College is named after George Brown, a journalist, politician, and one of the Fathers of Confederation in Canada.
